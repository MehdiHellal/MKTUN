History and multicultures Worth a Look

Located in the heart of North Africa, on the shimmering warm shores of the Mediterranean Sea, for millenia Tunisia has been the heart of the crossroads between Africa, Asia, and Europe. It's vibrant and diverse character is captured in the artisnal ceramics, rugs, and textiles.

TUNISIA!

Our Promise To You

MKTun is your gateway to the ancient lands of Greek and Roman lore, where Hannibal gathered his troops (and elephants), whose cultural fabric is woven with ancient Berber culture predating the Pharoahs and the classical artisery of the Arabs, where the Sahara desert meets the Sea. Tunisia is home to some of the world's most beautiful and quality artisnal products. From colorful ceramics crafted in Cap Bon to the intricately woven rugs of Kairouan, to the gleaming hand-carved olive woodware, Tunisia offers unique artisanal treasures. Browse through our content and dive into Tunisia's deep artisanal traditions.
